Meet The Scholars

Zach Harmon

PT, DPT

Co-Founder

Zach Harmon is a performance-minded problem solver who loves turning chaotic spaces into efficient, functional, and good-looking environments. As a physical therapist, strength coach, and entrepreneur, Zach has spent his career studying how people move, how spaces influence behavior, and how smart systems make life easier.


Garage Scholars was born from that mindset — blending organization, design, and practical utility into one streamlined service. Zach approaches every project like he approaches his clinics: with precision, clarity, and a commitment to long-term results.


His DPT background is the key differentiator for Garage Scholars' home gym installations. Every gym layout is designed with ergonomic equipment placement, safe training flow, and proper spacing — not guesswork from a YouTube video.


When he's not transforming garages, Zach is lifting, learning, or exploring the outdoors with his family.


Clear Space. Calm Mind. Confident Life.

Tyler Sodia

PhD

Co-Founder

Dr. Tyler Sodia is a systems-obsessed builder who loves turning big, messy ideas into clean, repeatable results. As a scientist, engineer, and entrepreneur, Tyler has spent his career studying how things work—materials, machines, workflows—and how small changes in environment can completely change how people feel and perform in their space.

His PhD background means every Garage Scholars project runs on systems, not luck. Detailed planning, trained crews, quality checkpoints, and a satisfaction guarantee that actually means something.


When he's not rethinking garages, Tyler is lifting, learning, or sketching out the next experiment on a whiteboard, a spreadsheet, or a napkin with a cup of coffee nearby and a new idea already in motion.
