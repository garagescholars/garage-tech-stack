Privacy Policy

At Garage Scholars, we take your privacy seriously. When you trust us to transform your garage, you're also trusting us with your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it when you use our website, request a quote, apply to join our team, or book any of our garage transformation services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that helps us serve you better and deliver our garage transformation services:

When you request a quote or book a service:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Home address and zip code (to confirm we serve your area in the Denver Metro)
  • Photos of your garage (uploaded through our quote form)
  • Details about your garage size and the services you're interested in (decluttering, organization, gym setup, or resale)

When you apply to join our team:

  • Name, email, and phone number
  • School and year (if applicable)
  • Availability, transportation info, and resume

When you subscribe to our newsletter:

  • Email address

Automatically when you visit our site:

  • Device and browser information
  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • General location (city/state level, not your exact address)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to run our business and provide you with the best garage transformation experience:

  • To provide our services: Schedule consultations, prepare quotes, coordinate garage cleanouts, gym installations, and resale pickups
  • To communicate with you: Follow up on quote requests, send appointment reminders, and provide project updates
  • To process team applications: Review scholar applications and schedule interviews
  • To send helpful content: Garage organization tips, service updates, and special offers (only if you subscribe to our newsletter — you can unsubscribe anytime)
  • To improve our website: Understand which pages are most helpful so we can make the experience better

3. How We Protect Your Information

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, which provides enterprise-grade encryption and security. Photos you upload for quotes are stored in secure cloud storage and are only accessed by our team to prepare your estimate. We never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Who Can Access Your Information

Only the Garage Scholars team (our co-founders and authorized scholars) can access your information, and only for the purpose of providing our services. We may share limited information with:

  • Service providers: Tools we use to run our business (email, scheduling, payment processing)
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

We will never sell your garage photos, contact information, or any personal data to advertisers or data brokers.

5. Your Garage Photos

When you submit photos through our quote request form, we use them solely to assess your garage and prepare an accurate estimate. Your photos are stored securely and are not shared publicly, used in marketing, or shown to anyone outside our team without your explicit permission.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • See your data: Request a copy of the personal information we have about you
  • Fix your data: Ask us to correct any inaccurate information
  • Delete your data: Ask us to remove your personal information from our systems
  • Unsubscribe: Opt out of marketing emails at any time

To exercise any of these rights, just email or call us — we'll take care of it promptly.

7. Cookies

Our website uses basic cookies to keep things running smoothly (like remembering your session). We don't use invasive tracking cookies or sell cookie data to advertisers.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are designed for homeowners and adults. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has submitted information through our site, please contact us and we'll remove it immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes to this Privacy Policy, we'll update this page. We encourage you to check back periodically, but we won't make any changes that reduce your privacy protections without giving you a heads up.
